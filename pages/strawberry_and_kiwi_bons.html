Strawberry and kiwi bons with chocolate

  • Preparation: 25 minutes
  • Cooking: 40 minutes

Strawberries are pure vitamin C, and kiwi contains a lot of fiber. If we also add a little chocolate, you get a powerful source of magnesium.
Well, so that you can enjoy all these benefits in a single dessert, you have to try these

INGREDIENTS

  • 200 g of strawberries
  • 3 kiwis
  • 100 g dark chocolate fondant
  • 40 g white chocolate fondant (optional)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Step 1. Wash and dry the strawberries and remove the leaves. Peel the kiwis and cut them into 4wedges.
  2. Step 2. Melt the dark chocolate in a bain-marie, stirring constantly.
  3. Step 3. Prick the fruits with a toothpick and dip them in the chocolate until the half its height. Place on baking paper and reserve in the refrigerator.

NUTRITION INFORMATION

Calories 150 calories | Total Fat 25 grams
Fiber 50 grams | Protein 25 grams
